How it works
One question. Two tracks.

Every question sent through HushAsk follows a dual-track routing system — delivered privately, documented permanently.

Track 01

Anonymous Delivery

DM the HushAsk bot or @mention it in any channel. HushAsk immediately hashes your identity and shows you two routing buttons — Public Knowledge or Confidential / HR. You choose where it goes. Your name never appears.

The recipient sees only the message — never the sender. Your Slack ID is SHA-256 hashed before anything is stored. Zero social risk for the employee.
Track 02

Notion Sync

When an answer is provided, HushAsk automatically syncs the Q&A pair to your connected Notion workspace. Questions that get asked once tend to get asked again — now they don't have to be.

Answered questions become permanent company assets. Your knowledge base grows every time someone speaks up — even anonymously.
What we stand for
Three guiding principles.
Value > Valuation.

We're not building for a pitch deck. HushAsk exists to solve a real problem that affects real teams every day: the question that goes unasked because asking feels risky. Every design decision is optimized for employee trust and organizational clarity — not growth metrics, not engagement loops.

UI > AI.

The most powerful feature of HushAsk is the one you don't notice: it disappears into your existing workflow. A DM. A Slack message. A Notion page. No dashboards to learn, no new habits to form. The interface is the product — and it should feel like it was always there.

Asset > Archive.

Every question asked in Slack is transient — here today, buried in chat history tomorrow. HushAsk changes that. When an answer is given, it's synced to Notion automatically. Transient chat becomes a permanent company asset. Your knowledge base grows every time someone speaks up.
